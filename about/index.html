Making philanthropy work

At Stoa, we want to help you find the right grants ‍We know your struggles well. Non profits, individuals, and businesses spend too much time on grants. Too much effort searching, too much human resources applying, too many months waiting to hear back. We’re given complicated application forms that aren’t centralized anywhere, and in existing grant databases, the information we’re looking for is paywalled, messy to sift through, or just poorly designed. The time we’re spending on repetitive grant applications should be used to further our mission. ‍ That’s why we created Stoa, so finding the right grants for you can be easy, transparent, understandable — and free. In the Stoa Directory, you can search for grants by topic areas and organizations, and sort for the newest grants or upcoming deadlines. Whether you’re working to find new solutions to climate change, or empowering youth in the community, there’s a grant out there for you. 

Let’s build tools to make philanthropy work, together

The world has evolved. Issues like climate change, racial reform, and public health pandemics have risen to the forefront of our minds, and technologies like mobile apps, collaboration software, and machine learning are weaved into everyday products we rely on. Yet, the philanthropy system has remained relatively unchanged. The burden of securing funding is still heavily on the changemakers on the ground and in our communities, and the majority of foundations are still working by themselves in their siloed networks. We believe good software can help the philanthropy world collaborate together.  If you’re a foundation, non-profit, individual, or business working to make a social impact, we’d love your experiences to inform our journey. Fill out this short form. If you’d like to lend a hand, please send us a note.
